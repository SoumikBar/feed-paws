About Our Mission

Bringing love and nourishment to every street corner

Our Story

Feed Paws Initiative was born from a simple yet powerful belief: every living being deserves love, care, and nourishment. Based in Raiganj, we work tirelessly to ensure that stray dogs and cats in our community never go hungry.

What started as a small effort has grown into a movement of compassion. We conduct regular feeding drives, provide medical care when needed, and work towards creating a more humane society for all animals.

Our vision extends beyond Raiganj - we dream of expanding our reach to help more animals across different regions, creating a network of care and compassion.
